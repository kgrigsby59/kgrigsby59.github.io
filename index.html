QuarterMinder for iPhone and iPad is a convenient way to keep track of your “50 State Quarters®”, “D.C. and U.S. Territories Quarters”, and “America the Beautiful Quarters®.”

Features:
• Multiple Collections
• Tracking for Denver and Philadelphia mints.
• Add notes for each coin
• Includes the latest “America the Beautiful Quarters®” with updates as they become available.
• Export to Message, Mail, Notes, and installed social media apps.
• Large detail images
• Link to Maps and U.S. Mint website for each quarter
• Upcoming Quarters widget
• Dynamic Type support
